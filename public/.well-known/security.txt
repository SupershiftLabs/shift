# Security Vulnerability Reporting

If you discover a security vulnerability within SuperShift Labs' website or services, 
please send an email to admin@supershiftlabs.com with details. We take all security 
reports seriously and will respond promptly.

Please include:
- Type of vulnerability
- Affected URL or endpoint
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Your contact information (optional)

We appreciate responsible disclosure and will acknowledge your contribution.
